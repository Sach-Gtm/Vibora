ABOUT VIBORA

India's boldest custom merchandise brand. Your Ideas of style Deserve to Exist.

OUR STORY

Vibora was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves to wear their story. Whether you're a startup founder wanting branded tees for your team, a college organizing a fest, or someone who wants a unique gift for a loved one — we make it happen.

We started as a small custom printing operation in India and grew into a full-service merchandise brand. Today, we serve 1000+ happy customers across India with our range of t-shirts, mugs, bottles, mouse pads, key chains, and corporate gift boxes.

Our mission? Make premium custom merch affordable for everyone. That's why we're at least 10% cheaper than competitors — without compromising on quality.
